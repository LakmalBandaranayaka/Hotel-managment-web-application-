Training Tips

Progressive Overload

Gradually increasing the weight or resistance in your workouts is key to muscle growth. Aim to challenge your muscles every week.

Consistency is Key

Maintaining a consistent workout schedule will help you see the best results. Try to train each muscle group at least twice a week.

Workout Routines

Push Workout

  • Bench Press - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Overhead Press - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Tricep Dips - 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press - 4 sets of 8-10 reps

Pull Workout

  • Pull-Ups - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Barbell Rows - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Deadlifts - 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Face Pulls - 4 sets of 10-12 reps

Leg Workout

  • Squats - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lunges - 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Press - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Calf Raises - 4 sets of 12-15 reps

Nutrition Advice

High Protein Diet

Protein is essential for muscle repair and growth. Include sources like chicken, fish, lean meats, and plant-based proteins in your diet.

Hydration is Important

Staying hydrated is crucial for performance and recovery. Aim to drink at least 3-4 liters of water per day, especially during intense workouts.

Stay Motivated
